“Nobody can fully understand the meaning of love unless he’s owned a dog.” – Gene Hill.

Weather you are a seasonal dog lover or a dedicated dog parent, you have found your digital home where wagging tails and wet noses reign supreme. The mission of ‘Walk with Tyson’ is to bring dog owners together for meetups and leisure walks around Dublin and surrounding areas such as Wicklow and Kildare, as dogs love their walks, enjoy checking out sights and smells. Further, if dogs don’t receive enough exercise, they can easily become bored and destructive. But are these walks just for your dogs??

Happy Puppy Healthy Puppy!!

The answer is NO! Exercise is of very much importance to humans to that of dogs. Just a 30 minutes’ walk every day can increase cardiovascular fitness, strengthen bones, reduce body fat and boost muscle power and endurance. In addition, even a short burst of 10 minutes of brisk walking increases mental alertness, energy and positivity of both you and your dog. Also, this is a wonderful opportunity to share each other’s knowledge and challenges in being a dog parent
